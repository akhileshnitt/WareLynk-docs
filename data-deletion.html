Data Deletion Policy

Last Updated: February 19, 2026 Effective Date: February 19, 2026

1. Overview

At WareLynk, we respect your right to control your personal data. This Data Deletion Policy explains how you can request the deletion of your account and personal data from our platform, what data will be deleted, what data is retained for legal and compliance purposes, and how long the process takes.

This policy is required by Google Play Store (Data Safety) and complies with India's Information Technology Act, 2000 and applicable data protection regulations.

2. How to Request Account and Data Deletion

To request deletion of your account and personal data, send an email to:

Deletion Request Email

📧 warelynk.official@gmail.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Include in your email:

  • Your full name
  • The email address registered with your WareLynk account
  • Your registered phone number
  • A brief statement confirming your request (e.g., "I request the permanent deletion of my WareLynk account and all associated personal data.")

3. Verification Process

To protect your account from unauthorized deletion requests, we will verify your identity before processing any deletion:

  1. We will send a verification code to your registered email address or phone number.
  2. You must confirm the code within 24 hours.
  3. Once verified, your deletion request will be queued for processing.

4. Processing Timeline

Stage Timeframe
Acknowledgment of your request Within 2 business days
Identity verification Within 2 business days of receiving your verification response
Account deactivation (login disabled) Within 7 days of verified request
Data deletion from active systems Within 30 days of verified request
Removal from backups Within 90 days

You will receive an email confirmation once your account and data have been deleted.

5. What Data Gets Deleted

Upon a successful deletion request, the following data will be permanently deleted from our active systems:

Personal Account Data

  • ✅ Name, email address, phone number
  • ✅ Profile photo
  • ✅ Password and authentication tokens
  • ✅ Device identifiers and push notification tokens

KYC Documents

  • ✅ Identity document images (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, etc.)
  • ✅ Business registration document images
  • ✅ KYC verification status and notes

Property Data (Owners)

  • ✅ Property listings, descriptions, and photos
  • ✅ Property availability and pricing data

Booking & Communication Data

  • ✅ Booking requests and history
  • ✅ Messages and notifications

Usage Data

  • ✅ Search history
  • ✅ In-app activity logs
  • ✅ Analytics identifiers linked to your account

6. What Data is Retained (and Why)

Some data must be retained after account deletion for legal, regulatory, and accounting purposes. This data is securely stored and isolated from active platform use.

Data Type Retention Period Reason
Transaction records (invoices, payments, POs) 7 years Indian income tax and GST compliance (Income Tax Act, GST Act)
KYC documents 5 years Anti-money laundering (AML) compliance and regulatory requirements
Dispute and legal records 3 years Legal obligation to preserve records relevant to active or potential disputes
Aggregated, anonymized analytics Indefinitely Non-identifiable data used for platform improvement (cannot be linked to you)

After the applicable retention period, all retained data is permanently deleted.

7. Effect of Account Deletion

Once your account is deleted:

  • You will no longer be able to log in to WareLynk
  • All your active property listings (if Owner) will be unpublished
  • Any pending bookings will be cancelled — we recommend resolving all pending bookings before submitting a deletion request
  • Any outstanding payments owed to you (if Owner) will be processed before deletion; please ensure your payment details are up to date
  • You will not receive any further push notifications or emails from WareLynk (except for legally required communications)
  • Your username/email may become available for re-registration after deletion is complete

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. If you wish to use WareLynk again after deletion, you will need to create a new account and complete KYC verification again.

8. Deletion of Data Shared with Third Parties

WareLynk uses the following third-party services that may hold copies of your data. We will request deletion from these services as part of your account deletion:

Service Data Held Deletion
Amazon Web Services (AWS S3) KYC documents, property photos Deleted within 30 days
Firebase (Google) Push notification tokens, crash logs, analytics Deleted within 30 days per Firebase's data deletion policy
Razorpay Payment transaction records Subject to Razorpay's retention policy (financial records may be retained 7+ years per RBI guidelines)

For questions about data held by these third parties, you may also contact them directly using their respective privacy/deletion request processes.
