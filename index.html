How It Works

Get started in minutes — for both owners and businesses

For Warehouse Owners
1

Sign Up & KYC

Create your account and complete KYC verification. Our admin team reviews and approves your profile.

2

List Your Property

Add your warehouse with photos, dimensions, location, amenities, and pricing.

3

Receive Booking Requests

Businesses find and request your space. Review requests and approve or decline with one tap.

4

Invoice & Get Paid

Generate invoices automatically. Receive secure Razorpay payments and track your earnings.

For Businesses
1

Sign Up & KYC

Register your business and complete KYC verification to unlock access to all verified warehouses.

2

Search & Discover

Browse warehouses by city, size, price, and amenities. View photos, location, and availability.

3

Book & Raise a PO

Send a booking request and raise a Purchase Order. Get confirmed once the owner approves.

4

Pay Securely

Pay via UPI, card, or net banking through Razorpay. Receive your invoice instantly after payment.
