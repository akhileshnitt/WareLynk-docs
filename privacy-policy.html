Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 19, 2026 Effective Date: February 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to WareLynk ("we", "our", or "us"). WareLynk is a warehouse and storage space rental platform that connects property owners with businesses seeking flexible storage solutions.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App") and related services (collectively, the "Services"). Please read this policy carefully. By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you register for and use WareLynk, we collect the following information:

Account Information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Password (stored in encrypted form)
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • User role (Property Owner or Business User)

KYC (Know Your Customer) Verification Information:

  • Government-issued identity documents (Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, Voter ID, or Driving License)
  • Business registration documents (GST Certificate, Incorporation Certificate)
  • Business address
  • Bank account details (for owners receiving payments)

Property Listings (for Owners):

  • Property name and description
  • Property address and location coordinates
  • Property photos and images
  • Property type, size, and amenities
  • Pricing information

Booking Information (for Business Users):

  • Booking dates and duration
  • Billing address
  • Special requirements or notes

Financial Information:

  • Payment details processed via Razorpay (we do not store full card numbers or CVV)
  • Billing and payment history
  • Invoice and Purchase Order records

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, we automatically collect:

Device Information:

  • Device type, model, and operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • Mobile network information

Location Information:

  • Precise GPS location (when you search for nearby warehouses or add a property)
  • Approximate location derived from IP address

Usage Data:

  • Features and screens accessed
  • Timestamps of activity
  • Search queries
  • App crash reports and diagnostics

Push Notification Tokens:

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token for sending push notifications

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from:

  • Google Sign-In: If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from your Google account.
  • Razorpay: Payment confirmation and transaction reference IDs (we do not receive or store full payment card details).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

3.1 Providing and Improving the Services

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Facilitating warehouse bookings between owners and businesses
  • Processing payments and generating invoices and purchase orders
  • Verifying your identity and business legitimacy through KYC
  • Displaying nearby warehouses based on your location
  • Sending booking confirmations, status updates, and notifications

3.2 Communication

  • Sending push notifications for booking requests, approvals, rejections, and updates
  • Sending transactional emails (booking confirmations, invoice receipts)
  • Responding to customer support inquiries

3.3 Security and Fraud Prevention

  • Verifying user identities through KYC documents
  • Detecting and preventing fraudulent activity
  • Enforcing our Terms of Service
  • Complying with legal obligations

3.4 Analytics and Product Improvement

  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve app features
  • Monitoring app performance and fixing bugs
  • Conducting internal research and analytics

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We share your information only in the following circumstances:

4.1 Between Users

  • Property details (name, description, location, photos, pricing) of approved listings are visible to all Business Users on the platform.
  • Business User details (name, contact information) are shared with Property Owners when a booking request is made.
  • Owner details (name, contact information) are shared with Business Users upon booking confirmation.

4.2 Third-Party Service Providers

We share data with trusted service providers who help us operate our Services:

Provider Purpose Data Shared
Amazon Web Services (AWS S3) Secure document and image storage KYC documents, property photos
Firebase (Google) Push notifications, analytics, crash reporting Device tokens, usage data
Razorpay Payment processing Billing information for transaction
Google Maps / Location Services Location-based warehouse search Location coordinates

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the specified purpose.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority, or when we believe disclosure is necessary to:

  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Protect the rights and safety of WareLynk, our users, or the public
  • Detect, prevent, or address fraud or security issues

4.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or part of our assets, user data may be transferred to the acquiring entity. We will notify you via email or a prominent notice in the App before your data is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Where We Store Data

Your data is stored on secure servers hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS) infrastructure, located in India or as determined by our infrastructure provider.

5.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between the App and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • Encryption at rest: Sensitive documents and files stored in AWS S3 are encrypted at rest.
  • Access controls: Only authorized personnel with a legitimate need can access personal data.
  • Authentication: Secure token-based authentication (JWT) for all API requests.

5.3 Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide Services. Specifically:

  • Account data: Retained for the duration of your account and up to 3 years after account deletion for legal and accounting purposes.
  • KYC documents: Retained for 5 years from the date of verification, as required by applicable law.
  • Transaction records (invoices, POs, payments): Retained for 7 years for accounting and tax compliance.
  • Usage logs: Retained for up to 12 months.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Correction

You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you and request corrections if it is inaccurate. You can update most of your information directly in the App under Profile → Edit Profile.

6.2 Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your account and personal data. To submit a deletion request:

  • Email: warelynk.official@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request"
  • Include: Your registered email address and phone number for verification

We will process your request within 30 days. Note that some data may be retained for legal and accounting obligations as described in Section 5.3. See our full Data Deletion Policy.

6.3 Withdraw Consent / Opt-Out

  • Push Notifications: You can disable push notifications at any time through your Android device settings.
  • Location Access: You can revoke location permissions through your Android device settings. Note that this may limit the warehouse search functionality.
  • Marketing Emails: You can opt out of non-transactional emails by contacting warelynk.official@gmail.com.

6.4 Data Portability

You may request a copy of your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format by contacting us at warelynk.official@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

WareLynk is intended for use by adults aged 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18. If we become aware that a child under 18 has provided us with personal information, we will promptly delete that information. If you believe a minor has used our Services, please contact us at warelynk.official@gmail.com.

8. Third-Party Links and Services

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services (e.g., payment pages, government portals). This Privacy Policy does not apply to those third-party services. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you use.

9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The WareLynk mobile app does not use browser cookies. However, we use similar tracking technologies such as:

  • Firebase Analytics for in-app usage analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics for crash reporting
  • Device identifiers for session management

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.
  • For material changes, we will notify you via a push notification or email at least 7 days before the changes take effect.
  • Your continued use of the App after the effective date constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of India, including the Information Technology Act, 2000, and the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011.
